About COC Directory

COC Directory is an improved version of the Church Of Christ Directory that was compiled by Oji O. Oji (Evang). This website serves as an easy way to search through the contact easily on any device without having to flip through pages of your manual.
However, it also allows users to easily search the contacts list of each congregation, send them mails and route calls instantly. There is need for brethren to keep in touch with one another easily and more conveniently. This is why we are making this a reality!
